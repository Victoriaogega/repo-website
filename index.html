My first week at AkiraChix


My experience

My first week at AkiraChix was very exciting and intresting.I learnt so many things and different cultures.I got to know how to do first aid and that there are different types of fire extinguishers depending on the type of fuel being burnt.I also learnt that Ethiopia is in the year 2017 and that the Ethiopians fast twice a week,on Wednesday and on Friday.Their fasting involves abstaining from animal products and not food. That was fascinating to me.
I ejoyed the warm shower and the clean bedding too.I made new friends and i also learned how to play foosbal(the table football) and now it is my favourite thing to do.

Good things that i expirienced

  1. Spacious room and bathroom
  2. The food
  3. Hospitality from the AkiraChix community

Not so good things that i expirienced

  • The weather (it is very cold)
  • Waking up very early
  • people talking in a language i dont understand despite being told not to
